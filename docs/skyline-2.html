Pass & Play mode — all data lives in this browser tab. Hand the device around. Each player sees only the public board; private tile hands are shown only on your turn.




Rules Overview
Goal: Have the highest net worth when the game ends. Net worth = cash + stock value + merger bonuses you'd earn.

On your turn:
1. Place a tile from your hand onto the board.
2. Resolve what the tile does (see below).
3. Buy up to 3 stocks total in any active hotel(s).
4. Draw a replacement tile to refill your hand to 6.

What a tile can do:
Isolated — sits alone, nothing happens.
Extend — joins an existing chain, growing it by 1+ tiles.
Found — connects two or more neutral tiles (or sits next to one) with no existing chain nearby. You choose which hotel opens and receive 1 free share.
Merger — connects two or more existing chains. See below.

Mergers:
The larger chain survives. If chains are tied in size, the player who placed the tile chooses the survivor.
The defunct chain's shareholders receive bonuses: the majority holder gets 10× the share price; the minority holder gets 5×. Ties split the combined amount evenly. The sole shareholder gets both.
Then, starting with the merger-maker and going clockwise, each shareholder of the defunct chain independently chooses what to do with their stock:
  💰 Sell — receive the pre-merger price per share in cash.
  🔄 Trade 2-for-1 — swap pairs of defunct shares for shares of the surviving chain.
  📦 Keep — hold the defunct shares; they're worthless unless that chain re-founds later.
If multiple chains are absorbed, each is resolved one at a time.

Safe chains (11+ tiles) cannot be acquired in a merger. Two safe chains cannot be merged.

Game ends when any chain reaches 41+ tiles, or all active chains are safe. Any player may declare the end on their turn. Final bonuses are paid on all active chains, then net worth determines the winner.
